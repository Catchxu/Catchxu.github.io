Greetings, welcome to my academic page!

I am Kaichen Xu, currently a fourth-year undergraduate student in Statistics at [Zhongnan University of Economics and Law (ZUEL)](https://english.zuel.edu.cn/), where I am privileged to work under the mentorship of [Prof. Xiaobo Sun](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=l56IWfgAAAAJ&hl=en) from the School of Statistics and Mathematics.

My research interests involve developing generative models and exploring their applications, including but not limited to anomaly detection and multimodal learning. On one hand, I am deeply passionate about utilizing statistical tools to develop theory-driven deep learning modules, enabling generative models to perform better on specific tasks. On the other hand, I firmly believe that generative models can do more than just generate content and have been continuously exploring their potential applications.

If you are interested in my work, please feel free to send me an e-mail at kaichenxu358@gmail.com and I am more than happy to discuss!
